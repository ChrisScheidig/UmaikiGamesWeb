SANTRIA! is a light-hearted, modern-setting RPG with a strong emphasis on exploration and character interactions, featuring a tactical battle system. Pine Falls is a small town in the US with numerous quirky characters you can socialize and romance with. Incidentally, you discover a conspiracy during your everyday school life, resulting in the awakening of your psychic abilities. The mystery plot is inspired by classic TV shows like Twin Peaks and X-Files.
