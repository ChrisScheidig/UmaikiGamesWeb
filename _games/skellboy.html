In a time where only love can bloom on the battlefield and all heroes are gone, glory from the past is the only way out of Kasputins malice intent. Help Skellboy to save the world (again?).

Skellboy is a short action-adventure and our first work in Unity5! It was created for another one month contest in October 2016. The final polished version will be released end of 2018!
